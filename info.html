B I O

LayreBoi is a very small independent music producer, started in 2019 and officially released tunes in the end of 2020 and befriended with Ako Shirakami, known as TTien63, who was formerly a member of a team MiraiCreative.

He first started by the name of DylanTube, releasing tunes on YouTube with feelings of getting failed, causing him to stop uploading any videos frequently and starting a new channel from scratch.

Before releasing any tunes on his new channel, LayreBoi has been practicing until he has met Ako. After he joined her Discord server, he decided to try to remix one of her tracks, Forever, reaching a total of two hundret views (today), then released his first release "Emotion Moonlight", a melodic dubstep music.
