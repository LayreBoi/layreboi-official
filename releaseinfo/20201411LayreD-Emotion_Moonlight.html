EMOTION MOONLIGHT

14th November 2020

My first release in YouTube and stores! I thought Melodic Dubstep should contain emotional melodies and strong drop and it came out ok, it's not pretty much popular...

Available at: SoundCloud, Spotify, Deezer and Amazon Music

This track was first released on YouTube. The music visualizer video took a long time to upload, due to multiple errors and retries... It was only available at YouTube and SoundCloud, until my friend, TTien63, teached me how to upload in Spotify.

Through Amuse, a free distributor, I've successfully released my track on stores, but not that successfully at first. After TTien63 told me what name I've put, I got scared because I've put my real name into the contributor list (songwriter), but she actually meant the artist name. After that scary time, my track got accidentally taken down by my request, while it was delivered to the stores, which was my "worst mistake". I've reuploaded my track to the distributor and it was approved again.
